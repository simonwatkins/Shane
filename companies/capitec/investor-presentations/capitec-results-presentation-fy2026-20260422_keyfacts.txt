GROUP KPIs (incl. AvaFin unless noted)
- Headline earnings: R16.8bn, +23% (FY2025: R13.7bn)
- Return on equity: 31% (FY2025: 29%)
- Credit loss ratio: 8.1% (FY2025: 7.5%) — stated "within through-the-cycle target"
- Net interest income: R24.080bn, +19% (FY2025: R20.185bn)
- Credit impairment charge: R9.974bn, +21% (FY2025: R8.258bn)
- Net interest income after credit impairments: R14.106bn, +18%
- Net transaction & commission income (incl. VAS & Connect): R21.525bn, +16% (FY2025: R18.535bn)
- Total net insurance income: R5.224bn, +38% (FY2025: R3.777bn)
- Non-interest income: R28.328bn, +19%; = 67% of income from operations after credit impairments (FY2025: 67%)
- Income from operations after credit impairments: R42.434bn, +19%
- Operating expenses: R20.238bn, +12% (FY2025: R18.061bn)
- Taxation: R5.342bn, +34%
- Fintech (VAS + Capitec Connect): R6.1bn, +38%

HEADLINE EARNINGS MIX (R16.8bn)
- Personal Banking 41% | Insurance 27% | Fintech (VAS + Connect) 26% | Business Banking 5% | AvaFin 1%

CLIENTS / VOLUMES
- 26m total active (+7%); 25.2m Personal Banking; 9.9m fully banked (+12%); 15m active on app (+19%); 12m VAS (+15%); 4.5m insurance (+11%)
- High earners (>R50k pm) +21%; 11m young adults (18-35), 54% market share
- Half of all payments now digital (+25%); e-commerce incl. Capitec Pay +32% to 643m txns; Pay wallets +103% to 335m txns; international/cross-border +29% to 85.6m txns

CREDIT
- Retail loan book growth 9.4%; Personal Banking loan disbursements +27% to R68.7bn; group total disbursements +34% to R98.3bn (per company press release)
- Credit Card book +32%; Credit Card sales +44%
- Personal Banking net transaction income = 62% of PB income after credit impairments

CAPITEC CONNECT (largest MVNO)
- 1.5m active clients in last 3 months (+67%); data usage 40.5 PB (+202%); R330m saved through lower data prices; launched device sales (Apple/Samsung)

INSURANCE
- Funeral Cover 16.6m lives (+13%); claims ratio 43.5% (from 44.9%); Life Cover lives +129% to 221 000
- 4.5m insurance clients

BUSINESS BANKING
- Businesses/entrepreneurs +71% to 456 000; merchant turnover R98.6bn (FY25: R63.9bn); scored lending book +118% to R3.1bn; scored disbursements +134% to R3.2bn

AvaFin (international consumer lending — Poland, Mexico, Czechia, Spain, Latvia; consolidated from 1 May 2024)
- Loan disbursements EUR629m (FY25: EUR474m partial)

BALANCE SHEET / CAPITAL
- NAV (total equity) R59.513bn (+17%); 116 099 843 ordinary shares
- CET1 / capital adequacy "well above regulatory minima"; healthy liquidity coverage; organic growth funded without capital raises (exact CET1 % in AFS — not in summary)
- 866 branches, 8 771 cash devices; headcount 17 236 (FY25: 16 525)
