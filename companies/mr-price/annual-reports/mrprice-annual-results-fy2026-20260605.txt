=== KEY POINTS ===
- Earnings dampened by expensing ALL once-off transaction costs for the acquisition of Pegasus Group Holding GmbH (trading as the retail business of NKD Group GmbH / "NKD"), which became effective POST year-end (i.e. recognised from FY2027).
- Cash sales = 89.4% of group retail sales (+4.4%); credit sales +3.5%; new accounts approved +11.0%. Group to continue managing credit book conservatively.
- 196 new stores opened across 15 trading chains; total footprint 3,182 stores; weighted-average trading space +3.6%.
- Omnichannel: >55% of online orders collected in store.
- Strong prior-year comparative base in H2 (prior-period retail sales +9.9%, boosted by two-pot retirement withdrawals).
- Macro: SARB raised rates 25bps at end-May 2026 (rate inflexion); household disposable income showed early signs of recovery in 2025 but discretionary demand stayed soft.
- CEO Mark Blair: "The agility of our operating model and the strength of our value retailing DNA have enabled operating leverage in a challenging retail environment... confident in our ability to perform across economic cycles."

=== COMPANY PROFILE (context) ===
- Cash-based, omnichannel, fashion-value retailer. ~15 trading chains across Apparel (Mr Price, Mr Price Sport, Miladys, Power Fashion, Studio 88) and Home (Mr Price Home, Sheet Street), plus Telecoms (Mr Price Cellular) and Financial Services; digital: Yuppiechef.
- Year-end: end-March/early-April (52/53-week retail calendar).
