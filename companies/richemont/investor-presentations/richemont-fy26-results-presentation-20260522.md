## Key figures
- Sales €22.4bn (+5% actual / +11% constant). Operating profit €4.5bn (+1% / +23% constant) incl. €164m non-recurring costs. Profit for year €3.5bn (py €2.8bn). Operating cash flow €4.9bn. Net cash €8.5bn. Capex €1.0bn (4.6% of sales). Communication spend 9% of sales.

## Sales by region (constant rates; % of sales)
- Americas +17% (10% of sales); Europe +9% (32%); Asia Pacific +8% (25%); Japan +9% (9%); Middle East & Africa +13% (?). Q4 by region: Americas +18%, Japan +28%, Europe +5%, Asia Pacific +14%, MEA -3% (March conflict).
- Sales bridge (€m): FY25 21,399 → Europe +366, Asia Pacific +54, Americas +444, Japan +43, MEA +114 → FY26 22,420.

## Channels (constant rates)
- DTC 77% of sales (Jewellery ~85%, SWM ~60%, Other >55%). Retail +12%; Online retail +8%; Wholesale incl. royalties +9%.
