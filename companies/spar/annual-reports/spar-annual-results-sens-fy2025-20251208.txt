=== KEY HIGHLIGHTS ===
- European strategic review largely completed; simplified Group; clearer forward earnings and capital allocation.
- Group net debt reduced 40% to R5.4bn (Prior period: R9.1bn); net debt leverage 1.74x at year-end. Southern Africa gearing improved to 1.75x.
- Group revenue, comparable basis, +1.6% over prior period (stronger H2 across both geographies). Constant-currency revenue +1.8%.
- Gross profit from continuing operations +3.3%; gross margins +20 bps to 10.8%.
- Operating profit (ex-extraordinary items) in Southern Africa, comparable basis, +6.8%.
- BWG Group (Ireland) operating profit margin above 3% despite -2.8% comparable operating profit.
- Cash generated from total operations +13.3% to R5.45bn (Prior: R4.81bn).
- Clear pathway to shareholder returns over short to medium term.

=== PERFORMANCE NOTES ===
- Full-year revenue +1.6% to R132.4bn; H2 FY2025 +3.5% (vs weak H1). Retailer loyalty 78.6% at end-Oct 2025 (Prior: 79.2%).
- Operating profit ex-extraordinary +2.3% to R2.8bn; group operating margin 2.1%.
- Net debt R5.4bn (Prior R9.1bn) on Switzerland/Poland disposals + working-capital management; gearing 1.74x (Prior 2.41x). SA interest cover 4.50x. Ireland leverage 1.71x, interest cover 11.19x.
- Elevated financing costs on legacy Poland debt assumed in SA → non-deductible interest → higher effective tax rate.
- Extraordinary items: impairments of corporate store goodwill and right-of-use assets in Southern Africa; impairment charges on SPAR Switzerland and AWG.
- Southern Africa: Groceries & Liquor sales +1.9% (H2 +2.9%); retail sales +2.4% (LFL +2.4%); Build it +2.4%; SPAR Health +13.2% (Scriptwise + wholesale). On-demand volumes +136% y/y; Uber Eats partnership from March 2025.
- Pet Storey: acquired Pet Masters Group; brand launched Sept 2025; all 12 stores converted by end-Nov 2025; first franchisee launched.
- BWG Group (Ireland ex-AWG): revenue EUR1.74bn (+0.6%); GP margin 13.7% (Prior 13.5%); foodservice gaining share.
- Sri Lanka JV: footfall +15%, items sold +6%; 11 new independent retailers + 1 corporate store added.

=== SHAREHOLDER DISTRIBUTION ===
No dividend declared for FY2025 (Prior: nil). Board prioritising further de-gearing. Committed to reinstating shareholder returns (dividends or share repurchases) in the short to medium term; reassessed regularly.

=== OUTLOOK ===
- Risk-controlled SAP rollout to safeguard supply chain and franchise continuity.
- Margin restoration in Southern Africa a priority (retailer programmes, category optimisation, cost discipline).
- Leverage expected to improve further; simplify capital structure to reduce interest drag, normalise tax burden, enhance FCF.
