COMMENTARY (verbatim highlights):
- Group: 29% growth in normalised operating profit to R15 210m; cash conversion now 77%; HE and normalised HE both +30%; normalised ROE up to 15.4% from 13.5%.
- EV increased to R126.6bn, a 15.7% RoEV; increasing contribution from non-covered businesses; favourable exchange rates and economic basis assumptions in aggregate.
- Total new business API declined 2% but +8% excluding the large Sasolmed take-on in the prior year.
- Capital ratios remained strong across every regulated business; debt across the Group decreased; FLR reduced to 16.8%. PAHI paid its second consecutive annual dividend at a 35% payout (previously 30%).
- "Discovery has emerged strongly from its cycle of significant investment ... positioned the Group for a new distinct phase of scaled organic growth, with focused execution through its recently formed global composite, Vitality, and its domestic business, Discovery South Africa."

DISCOVERY SOUTH AFRICA (22% growth in normalised profit from operations):
- Discovery Bank: "continued to deliver high-quality growth. Total clients increased by 30%, advances by 39% and deposits by 26%, resulting in strong revenue growth, and its first profitable period during the second half of the financial year, ahead of plan."
- Discovery Health: operating profit +7%, with further investment into technology, innovation and AI.
- Discovery Life: normalised operating profit +14% mainly driven by an exceptional claims experience. New business declined 10% in total and 2% for Individual Life.
- Discovery Invest: +29% operating profit on strong growth in AUM and certain one-off benefits.
- Discovery Insure: pricing/claims management + benign weather → significant claims-ratio improvement and +229% operating profit.

VITALITY (70% growth in normalised profit from operations):
- VitalityHealth: profits +173%, driven by effective pricing actions, a stabilising claims environment and rigorous claims and expense management.
- VitalityLife: operating profit recovered strongly, +70%, new business +28%.
- Ping An Health Insurance (PAHI): strong operating and investment result, +22% pre-tax profit, notwithstanding a sizeable COVID-19 reserve release in the prior year. Discovery's equity-accounted share, after tax and costs, +7% (prior year benefited from a tax gain).
- Vitality Network: integrated new business API by partners +24% to almost US$2 billion. Earnings +4% (7% in US$).
- VHI-Other: losses improved 25% to R291m, following lower losses in Vitality USA and successful integration of WellSpark Health.

DIVIDEND: Final gross cash dividend 201.00000 cents (160.80000 c net of 20% DWT) per ordinary share, out of income reserves. Shares in issue at declaration: 679 680 841. LDT 14 Oct 2025; ex 15 Oct 2025; record 17 Oct 2025; payment 20 Oct 2025.
