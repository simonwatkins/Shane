DISCOVERY LIMITED — H1 FY2026 INTERIM RESULTS (six months ended 31 December 2025)
Results presented 3 March 2026 (webcast https://www.corpcam.com/Discovery03032026).
NOTE: Quick-mode light touch. Figures below are from a credible SECONDARY source (Moonstone Information Refinery, 9 March 2026, reporting Discovery's interim results), NOT yet verified against the primary interim booklet/SENS (full interim analysis flagged as a coverage gap).
Source: https://www.moonstone.co.za/discovery-posts-strong-interim-results-as-bank-turns-profitable/

GROUP (H1 FY2026 vs H1 FY2025):
- Normalised operating profit: +24% to R8.9bn
- Headline earnings: +29% to R5.69bn
- Normalised headline earnings: +27% to R5.75bn
- ROE: 17.4%
- New business API: +12% to R14bn
- Embedded value: rose to R135.8bn (vs R126.6bn at 30 Jun 2025; R119.6bn at 31 Dec 2024)
- Discovery SA: normalised operating profit R6.78bn, +19%; new business +10% to R9.94bn; SA client base +7% to 6.5m

DISCOVERY BANK (the key turnaround — "profitability inflection point"):
- Normalised profit from operations: R75m profit, vs a R145m loss in the prior period (H1 FY2025)
- Operating profit before new business acquisition costs: R319m, from R69m (~+R220m YoY)
- Clients: +28% to 1.4m; accounts +29% to 3.37m
- Retail deposits +21%; advances +42%; non-interest revenue +39%
- Adding ~1 500 new customers/day; ~70% of new clients are NOT existing Discovery members
- Next phase: evolve into a "super bank" integrating health/insurance/investment products

OTHER SEGMENTS (H1 FY2026):
- Discovery Health: operating profit R2.13bn, +5% (would have been ~11% but for a R125m write-off of an ATB pharmacy-claims administrative error affecting >16 000 DHMS members; Discovery chose to absorb the cost). New business API +16% to R5.45bn; lives under administration 3.86m.
- Discovery Life: operating profit +15% to R3.09bn; RoEV 31% (≈14% excluding lower long-term interest rates); favourable mortality experience.
- Discovery Invest: operating profit +1% to R984m (ALM loss of R87m from interest-rate moves; underlying ~+21% excluding); AUA +20% to R200bn; AUM +24% to R142bn.
- Discovery Insure: operating profit +34% to R546m; operating margin 15.2%.

MANAGEMENT (Gore): "an excellent, strong, and robust period for our group"; group "continues to move ahead of its five-year growth targets". Launched Vitality AI (partnership with Google).
