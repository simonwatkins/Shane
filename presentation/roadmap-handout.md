# Claude at AllWeather Capital — Adoption Roadmap

*One-page summary for leadership · June 2026*

**The case:** JSE coverage currently spends analyst hours finding, downloading
and re-keying company documents before analysis can begin. The pilot workspace
automates that entire pipeline — discovery, download, cataloguing, and
first-pass analysis with every number cited to its source document and page —
so the team starts at the judgement stage, not the retrieval stage.

| Milestone | Scope | When | Success looks like |
|---|---|---|---|
| **M1 — JSE research workspace** | Deploy the demonstrated workspace to a SharePoint library; 2 pilot analysts; watchlist = current coverage (10 names live today) | Weeks 1–2 | Pilots answer "what did X report?" from the workspace, not from manual downloads |
| **M2 — Connect the estate** | SharePoint MCP for internal models and notes; Outlook for broker research; team-wide read access | Weeks 3–6 | Claude cites internal models alongside public filings |
| **M3 — Automate the rhythm** | Scheduled morning updates (new SENS/results across the watchlist); IC pack generation (PPTX/XLSX); analysts writing their own skills | Weeks 6–10 | Morning update in inboxes before the open; first analyst-authored skill in use |
| **M4 — Firm-wide rollout** | Beyond research: ops, compliance checks, IR monitoring; skill library governance; all-staff training | Q3 2026 | Each function owns at least one production skill |

**Guardrails built in from day one:** every figure cited to document and page;
estimates explicitly marked; outputs framed as informational, not advice;
closed-period flags; folder-scoped access with explicit permission prompts;
human review before anything leaves the building.

**Asks today:** (1) nominate two pilot analysts; (2) approve creation of the
SharePoint "JSE Research" library and connector consent; (3) 30-minute
follow-up in week 2 to review pilot output.

**Costs:** Claude Team/Enterprise licences for pilots now, broader seats at M4;
setup is roughly half a day of IT time. Detailed pricing in the appendix slide.
