## Disambiguation
GFI = **Gold Fields Limited** (SA-headquartered global gold miner). Do NOT confuse with
**Gold Road Resources** (ASX: GOR - the Gruyere JV partner GFI bought out in 2025), nor with
AngloGold Ashanti, Harmony or Sibanye-Stillwater. Verify any figure traces to a goldfields.com
document or a GFI SEC Form 6-K / SENS before use.

## Analytical Notes
- **Value driver:** earnings ~= (realised gold price - AISC) x oz. Highly leveraged to the
  US$ gold price; a weaker ZAR/AUD lowers US$ costs (rand hedge).
- **FY2025 anchors:** rev US$8,751m; adj EBITDA US$5,637m; attributable profit US$3,567m
  (incl. ~US$808m one-off Gruyere gain - use **normalised** US$2,684m for the clean read);
  AISC US$1,645/oz; attributable production 2.438Moz; adj FCF US$2,970m; net debt 0.26x.
- **FY2026 guidance:** 2.40-2.60Moz; AISC US$1,800-2,000/oz; capex US$1.9-2.1bn.
- **Watch:** Salares Norte run-rate (525-550koz, AISC <US$600); Windfall FID (mid-2026);
  Tarkwa lease renewal (expires Apr 2027) & Ghana royalty regime; group AISC vs guidance.
- **Returns policy:** 35% of normalised FCF as ordinary dividend (min US$0.50/sh) + up to
  US$500m specials/buybacks over 24 months.
