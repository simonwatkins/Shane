## ⚠️ Disambiguation (most common error)
There are TWO unrelated "Woolworths". This skill is **Woolworths Holdings, JSE: WHL**
(South African premium retailer). The **Australian Woolworths Group (ASX: WOW)** is a
separate supermarket company. Web searches for "Woolworths 2026 outlook / growth strategy"
frequently return WOW material (AU$71bn revenue, Auburn fulfilment centre, marketplace
250k SKUs, AU food EBIT 5.9%) — DISCARD all of it. Verify any figure traces to a
woolworthsholdings.co.za document or a WHL-specific SENS before using it.

## Sector-Specific Metrics (Retail — General + Food)
Primary: turnover & concession sales growth (report **constant-currency** too, given CRG/AUD),
comparable-store (like-for-like) growth, selling-price inflation vs CPI, gross-profit margin
(bps), adjusted EBIT/EBITDA and margins, **HEPS and adjusted diluted HEPS** (WHL leads on
adj. diluted HEPS), space growth, online contribution. Secondary: ROCE vs WACC, net debt/EBITDA,
cash conversion, dividend payout (target ~70% of headline earnings). Report **by division**:
Woolworths Food, Woolworths FBH, Woolworths Financial Services (book growth + impairment rate),
Woolworths Ventures, and Country Road Group (in AUD and ZAR).

## Known Quirks
- **Two "Woolworths"** — see disambiguation above. Single biggest research error.
- **AUD translation:** ~15% of group turnover is Country Road Group in AUD. Always state
  constant-currency vs reported, and remember a weaker ZAR/AUD inflates CRG's ZAR contribution
  while a weaker AUD/USD inflates CRG's own imported input costs.
- **Adjusted diluted HEPS** is management's preferred earnings metric; reported HEPS and EPS can
  diverge sharply from it on one-offs (e.g. H1 FY2025 included the Bourke St Melbourne property
  sale + rental, depressing H1 FY2026 EPS growth to −32.1% while HEPS was +9.6%).
- **52 vs 53-week years:** FY2024 was a 53-week year and included David Jones (since sold);
  use management's continuing-operations / pro-forma 52-week comparatives for like-for-like.
- **Analyst_Presentation.pdf** filename is not period-stamped on the server — rename on download.
- **Direct PDF binaries** may be blocked by some sandbox proxies; the SENS mirror
  (senspdf.jse.co.za) or web-fetch text extraction are reliable fallbacks.

## Analytical Notes Worth Pre-loading
- SA Food is the profit engine (~65% of sales, ROCE >40%, consistent market-share gains;
  Woolies Dash / online ~7%+ of SA Food and growing >20%).
- FBH thesis hinges on gross-margin recovery after DC-transition and price-investment drag.
- CRG turnaround is early and thin-margin (H1 FY2026 op. margin 2.6%); key risk item.
- Capital allocation: ~70% payout + active share buyback (commenced Sep 2025).
