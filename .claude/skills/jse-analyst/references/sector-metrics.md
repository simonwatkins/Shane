# Sector-Specific Metrics Reference

Loaded by `jse-analyst` (and referenced by company skills) to apply the right framework
for each sector. Add these to the general standardised metrics table.

## Banking

Primary: Net interest income (NII), Non-interest revenue (NIR), Credit loss ratio,
Cost-to-income ratio, CET1 capital ratio, ROE, Net interest margin (NIM), Advances
growth, Deposit growth.

Secondary: Stage 3 loans as % of gross loans, Specific impairment coverage ratio,
Liquidity coverage ratio (LCR), Net stable funding ratio (NSFR), Number of active
clients, Digital adoption rate, Cost of equity.

## Retail (Food)

Primary: Like-for-like sales growth, Selling price inflation vs CPI, Trading profit
margin, Inventory days, Space growth (sqm), Online sales contribution %, Shrinkage as %
of sales.

Secondary: Sales per square metre, Customer count growth, Basket size growth, Private
label penetration, Supply chain cost as % of sales, Store count by format.

## Retail (General / Clothing)

Primary: Like-for-like sales growth, Gross margin, Markdown rate, Inventory days, Space
growth, Online sales %, Credit sales as % of total.

Secondary: Concession income, Bad debt as % of credit book, Active account growth,
Returns rate, Average selling price movement.

## Mining

Primary: All-in sustaining cost (AISC) per unit, Production volumes, Reserve and resource
statement, Commodity price realised vs benchmark, Capex per unit, Adjusted EBITDA margin.

Secondary: Reserve replacement ratio, Ore grade, Recovery rate, Cash build-up /
(drawdown), Community and social investment spend, Carbon intensity per tonne, Fatality
and injury rates.

## Telco

Primary: ARPU (average revenue per user), Subscriber growth (prepaid and contract), Data
revenue as % of service revenue, Churn rate, Capex/revenue ratio, EBITDA margin.

Secondary: Data traffic growth, 4G/5G subscriber penetration, Tower count, Mobile money
users and transaction value (if applicable), Spectrum holdings.

## Property (REITs)

Primary: Distribution per unit, NAV per unit, Premium/discount to NAV, Portfolio vacancy
rate, Weighted average lease expiry (WALE), Cost-to-income ratio.

Secondary: Lease renewal rate, Escalation achieved, Loan-to-value (LTV), Weighted average
cost of debt, Interest cover ratio, Portfolio GLA, Development pipeline.

## Insurance

Primary: Value of new business (VNB), Embedded value (EV), Combined ratio (short-term),
Solvency margin, Claims ratio, Expense ratio.

Secondary: Persistency rates, New business margin, Return on embedded value (ROEV),
Underwriting margin, Investment return on shareholder funds.

## Industrials

Primary: Revenue by segment, EBITDA margin by division, Order book / backlog, Capex cycle
position, Working capital as % of revenue.

Secondary: Return on net operating assets (RONOA), Organic vs acquisition growth, Contract
renewal rate, Geographic revenue split, Headcount and revenue per employee.
